About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a city rich in culture, history, and modernity. Known as the "City of Pearls" and "City of Nizams", it beautifully blends tradition with technology.

Famous landmarks include the Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Hussain Sagar Lake. Hyderabad is also famous for its delicious Biryani and IT hubs like Cyberabad.

Hyderabadi Cuisine

Hyderabad is world-famous for its rich and spicy cuisine. The iconic Hyderabadi Biryani is a must-try, along with dishes like Haleem, Kebabs, and Double Ka Meetha.

Culture & Festivals

Hyderabad celebrates a blend of Hindu, Muslim, and Telugu cultural festivals. Major events include Diwali, Eid, Bonalu, and Sankranti. Music, dance, and handicrafts reflect the city's vibrant heritage.
